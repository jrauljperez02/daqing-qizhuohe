About Daqing Qizhuohe Economic and Trade Co.

Company Profile

Daqing Qizhuohe Economic and Trade Co., Ltd settled in Daqing city in 2021 under the national policy of revitalize Northeast agriculture. The company is mainly dedicated to import alfalfa and mung beans from high quality, various food crops and additives. Has established cooperative relationships long-term with companies exporting food and grain in the United States, Myanmar, Argentina, among others. Too with customs clearance from the port of Qingdao, customs declaration, sales to agricultural, grazing and processing businesses and the main wholesale markets of all the country. In 2022, the company helps in politics national industry revitalization dairy through actions to develop alfalfa, pilot of grain reform and feeding, and animal husbandry experiments pastoral. Trade cooperation has been negotiated with recognized alfalfa producing areas in Italy, Argentina and other countries to increase alfalfa and animal feed imports quality to meet domestic demand. Qizhuohe Economic and Trade Co., Ltd. has a practical professional management team and employee. Tianjin port and port of Qingdao have a sales team of more than 20 people to provide service guarantees to the clients. New customers can get in touch for inquiries and propose negotiations.
